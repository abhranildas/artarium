About Us

Artarium aims to provide a free platform to showcase and sell photography by amateur and professional artists to a wide audience of art lovers in India. We fulfill each order on behalf of the photographers: from printing, framing and shipping, to collecting payments from the buyers and sending royalty to the artists.
